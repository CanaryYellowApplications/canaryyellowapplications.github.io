Support

If you need help with a Canary Yellow Applications product, have a question, or want to report a problem, please contact us at support@canaryyellowapplications.com.

We use this email address for general support inquiries related to our applications and website.

When Contacting Support

If possible, please include a few helpful details in your message:

  • the name of the app or product you are asking about;
  • a short description of the issue or question;
  • what device or platform you are using; and
  • any steps that help explain how the issue occurred.

General Note

We will do our best to review support requests and respond as reasonably as possible, but response times may vary.
