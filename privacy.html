Privacy Policy

Canary Yellow Applications LLC ("we," "our," or "us") does not collect, request, store, or sell personally identifiable information through its applications.

We do not operate user data servers, we do not ask users to create accounts, and we do not ask for personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, mailing addresses, or payment information.

Information We Do Not Collect

Our apps do not collect personal information. They do not ask you to sign up, log in, or submit identifying details. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, mailing addresses, or payment details.

We also do not store user data on our own servers, because we do not maintain servers for that purpose. The apps are intended to work locally on your device rather than by sending your information to us.

Device Permissions

Some of our apps may request access to certain device features, such as the camera, photos, files, notifications, or location, if those features are needed for the app to work as intended.

  • Camera access may be used for photo capture or camera-based features.
  • Location access may be used for location-based functionality, if relevant.
  • Photo or file access may be used so you can open, save, import, or export content.

These permissions allow the app to use features on your device. They do not mean that we receive, collect, or store that information on our servers.

How Information Is Used

If an app accesses content or device features, that access is used only to provide the feature you chose to use. We do not use personal information for advertising, profiling, or data brokerage.

Third-Party Services

While Canary Yellow Applications LLC does not collect personal data through its apps, app stores and platform providers such as Apple or Google may handle downloads, purchases, diagnostics, or other platform-level services under their own terms and privacy policies. Their practices are separate from ours.

Data Storage and Security

Because we do not collect or store personal information on our own systems, there is no personal user database maintained by Canary Yellow Applications LLC. Where app data exists, it is intended to remain on your device.

Changes to This Policy

If our practices change in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy so it continues to accurately describe how our apps operate.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or a specific Canary Yellow Applications product, please contact us through the support information provided on this website.
