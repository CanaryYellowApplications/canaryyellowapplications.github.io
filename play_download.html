How to use your Google Play promo code

If you received a promo code for one of our Android apps, redeem it in Google Play with the Google Account you want to use for the app or subscription.

Redeem your code in Google Play

  1. Open the Google Play Store app on your Android phone or tablet.
  2. Tap your profile picture in the top-right corner.
  3. Tap Payments and subscriptions, then Redeem code.
  4. Enter your promo code and tap Redeem.
  5. Review the Google Account that will receive the redemption, then confirm.
  6. Download or open the app from Google Play and complete any remaining in-app steps if prompted.

Important note

Some codes apply at checkout For certain subscriptions, Google may ask you to enter the code during the in-app or Play checkout flow instead of before installation.
Use your personal Google Account Work or school-managed Google accounts can block redemption features.
Country restrictions apply Google Play codes can only be redeemed in the country or region where they were issued.

Troubleshooting

  • If the code does not work, enter it without spaces or dashes.
  • If Google says the code is not valid in your country, confirm your Play country matches the code’s region.
  • If the app uses a subscription promo code, start the subscription flow in the app and look for Redeem code in the payment section.
  • If you keep seeing an account-related error, switch to a personal Google Account and try again.

These steps reflect current Google Play Help guidance for redeeming gift and promotional codes on Android.
