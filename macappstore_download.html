How to use your Mac promo code

If you received a promo code for one of our Mac apps, redeem it in the Mac App Store. The app will be attached to the Apple Account that is signed in on your Mac.

Redeem your code

  1. Open the App Store on your Mac.
  2. Click your name in the lower-left corner, or click Sign In if you are not already signed in.
  3. Click Redeem Gift Card.
  4. Enter your promo code manually, then click Redeem.
  5. If prompted, sign in again with the Apple Account you want associated with the app.
  6. After redemption, download or open the app from your account’s purchased items.

What to expect

Redeem on the Mac itself Use the App Store app on macOS instead of trying to redeem a Mac app code from an iPhone or iPad.
Type the code in manually Apple’s Mac guidance for download codes supports direct manual entry in the App Store.
New Mac setup can delay offers On a newly set up Mac, some promotions can take a little time to appear after sign-in.

Troubleshooting

  • If Redeem Gift Card is missing, make sure you are signed in to the App Store first.
  • Check that your Mac meets the app’s minimum macOS requirements.
  • If the code fails, restart the App Store or sign out and back in before trying again.
  • If the code appears invalid, verify each character carefully. Some letters and numbers are easy to confuse.

These steps are based on current Apple Support instructions for redeeming download codes in the Mac App Store.
